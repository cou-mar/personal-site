About Me

I'm a junior Web Developer with a legal background who is looking for a chance to demonstrate I have what it takes to learn, grow, and excel in this field.

I'm most motivated by passion, justice, human rights, and interpersonal connections. As such, I desire to do meaningful work. My aim is to join the tech industry and leverage my skills in order to impact the world at large. Despite not having prior experience in tech, I am a quick learner who takes initiative and hungers to succeed.

I aspire to work for an organization or company that cares about its impact on society as well as the development of its employees. If it's willing to invest in me, as a newcomer to the industry, then I will give it everything I can. Work-life balance is important to me in addition to the overall culture of the company. The flexibility of remote and in-person work would be ideal.

Portfolio

Rally for Pink

This full-stack single-page web app was created using the MERN stack. It's an event tracker specifically for events related to breast cancer. I chose this topic as a dedication to my aunt who passed two days prior to starting this project. Users can view existing events, add new ones, and save events to their "favorites" list.

HTML · CSS · Mongoose · MongoDB · ExpressJS · React · NodeJS · Axios

The Wanderer's Guide

This full-stack multi-page web app organizes traveling by allowing you to create and save either lists (in numbered format e.g. travel checklist) or notes (in free-form text format e.g. journal entry).

HTML · CSS · Mongoose · MongoDB · ExpressJS · React · NodeJS · HandlebarsJS

Waste Less

This canvas-based game aims to educate players about proper recycling methods in a fun way. Players move the bin using arrow keys to collect recyclable material. Negative points are given for placing incorrect items in the bin.

HTML · CSS · JavaScript
